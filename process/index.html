Google Presence

Show up where customers are actually looking

When someone searches for a restaurant, auto shop, or service business near them, the results they see aren't just websites — they're Google Business Profiles. A well-configured listing with accurate hours, photos, and the right categories gets you in front of customers before they ever visit a website.

We set up or clean up your Google Business Profile as part of onboarding, and build your website with the local SEO foundations that help search engines understand who you are, where you are, and who you serve.

  • Google Business Profile creation or full audit and cleanup
  • Accurate business categories, hours, and service area configuration
  • Photo and description optimization for local search
  • Schema markup on your site so Google reads your business data correctly
  • Sitemap submitted to Google Search Console
  • Page titles, descriptions, and structure built for local keyword relevance
  • Guidance on building Google reviews from real customers

  • ✓
