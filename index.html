Operating Model

How Fused Operates

Every venture inside Fused Distribution runs through the same four-step operator loop. It's what keeps things direct, predictable, and expansion-ready — whether we're shipping a website, a silver order, or a box of miniatures.

1. Build

Every venture starts operator-led — a site, a supply chain, a curriculum, or a new catalog line.

2. Source

Relationships stay direct — Cloudflare for hosting, bullion producers for silver, wholesale networks for collectibles. No middlemen drag.

3. Deliver

Customers get something real and fast. Websites go live in days. Silver ships monthly. New drops land as they're ready.

4. Expand

The roster grows. New clients, new products, new verticals — continuously, as sourcing advantages and customer needs open up.
