Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Fused Direct Distribution ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and handles information you provide when you use this website (fuseddistribution.com), including its sub-pages at /reserve/ and /projects/.

Information We Collect

We only collect information you voluntarily submit through our contact form, which includes:

  • Full name
  • Company name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Your message or inquiry

We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics tools. We do not collect information passively as you browse this site.

How We Use Your Information

Information submitted through our contact form is used solely to:

  • Respond to your business inquiry
  • Follow up on sourcing or distribution requests
  • Contact you about the specific service or product you asked about

We do not use your information for marketing, advertising, or any purpose beyond responding to your inquiry.

How Your Information Is Transmitted

Form submissions are delivered to us by email via Resend, a third-party email delivery service. Your submitted data is transmitted securely over HTTPS and processed by Resend solely for the purpose of delivering it to us. Resend's privacy policy is available at resend.com/legal/privacy-policy.

Data Retention

We retain inquiry information only as long as necessary to respond to and manage your request. We do not maintain a marketing database or CRM populated from this form.

We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with any third party for their marketing or commercial purposes.

Your Rights

Depending on where you are located, you may have rights to access, correct, or request deletion of personal information we hold about you. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the form on the main page.

Security

All traffic to this site is encrypted via HTTPS. We implement standard security measures including HTTP security headers to protect against common web vulnerabilities. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For questions about this policy or how we handle your information, use the contact form at fuseddistribution.com/#contact.
