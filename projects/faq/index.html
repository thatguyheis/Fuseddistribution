Pricing & Plans

Foundation ($99/mo) gets you a custom site, hosting, a contact form, and one content update per month — the essentials done right. Standard ($300/mo) adds Google Business setup, local SEO, and more frequent updates. Expansion ($500/mo) includes everything — unlimited updates, AI workflow integration, 1:1 coaching, and dedicated account management. See the full breakdown on the Pricing page.

Monthly plans are month-to-month — cancel any time with no penalty and no questions asked. Annual plans are billed upfront at a discounted rate, but we're transparent about what that covers before you commit. We'd rather earn your business every month than lock you into something that isn't working.

Depending on your plan, you get 1–3 content updates per month, or unlimited on the Expansion plan. Just send us what needs to change — updated hours, a new menu item, a seasonal promotion, a new team member — and we handle the rest. No tickets, no back-and-forth portals. We keep it simple.

Hosting & Ownership

You own your content and your domain — those are always yours. The site is built and hosted on infrastructure we manage as part of the subscription. If you ever decide to leave, we'll help you export or migrate your content so you're never left without options.

Hosting runs on Cloudflare's global network — fast, secure, and built for reliability. SSL certificates, security headers, uptime monitoring, and fast page load performance are all included in every plan. There's no separate hosting bill and no hidden fees layered on top.
